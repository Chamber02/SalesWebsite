Would you like to track and monitor your vehicle directly from your smartphone?

Question 4 of 10

Would you like a dashcam that records everything happening on the road in front of you?

A road-facing dashcam captures video evidence of accidents, near-misses and road incidents, useful for insurance claims and your own peace of mind.

Question 5 of 10

Question 6 of 10

Would you like automatic crash detection that calls for help if you're in an accident plus roadside and medical assistance at the touch of a button?

If your car detects a collision, Cartrack immediately alerts emergency services and your emergency contacts even if you cannot call for help yourself.

Question 7 of 10

Would you like your trips automatically logged, and be notified if your Cartrack device is disconnected from your vehicle's battery?

The logbook automatically records every trip and separates personal from business travel. The power event notification alerts Cartrack's 24/7 control room the moment your tracking device loses power from the vehicle battery , an early warning sign of tampering or attempted theft.

Question 8 of 10

Would you like to be instantly alerted if your car moves, starts or is tampered with even when you are nowhere near it?

CarWatch is Cartrack's early-warning system. Activate it with a tap, and Cartrack will contact you immediately if your vehicle moves without your knowledge giving you a head start if your car is stolen.

Question 9 of 10

Would you like a hidden backup tracking device that keeps working even if a thief tries to jam or disable your main tracker?

The Cartrack Tag is a small wireless anti-jamming backup unit discreetly installed on your vehicle. It provides a vital second layer of protection in areas with poor signal or active jamming . Already included in all bike packages.

Question 10 of 10

Would you like all your Cartrack devices covered for repairs and replacements at no extra cost for the life of your subscription?

The Lifetime Maintenance Warranty means if any Cartrack device develops a fault or needs replacing, Cartrack takes care of it with no call-out fees, no excess and no hidden charges, for as long as you subscribe.

Question 1 of 10

Would you like real-time fuel level monitoring and consumption reporting across your fleet?

Fuel Monitoring lets you track fuel levels and usage per vehicle in real time to help identify theft, excessive idling and inefficiency across your entire fleet.

Question 2 of 10

Does your fleet transport temperature-sensitive goods that need to be monitored throughout transit?

Temperature Monitoring uses in-vehicle probes to track cargo temperature in real time , ensuring compliance, product integrity and cold chain visibility.

Question 3 of 10

Would you like to know exactly which driver is operating each vehicle at any given time?

Driver Identification links each trip to a specific driver , eliminating timesheet disputes and improving accountability.

Question 4 of 10

Would you like to prevent unauthorised drivers from starting your fleet vehicles entirely?

Driver Identification + Start Prevent combines driver ID with vehicle immobilisation. If an unrecognised driver attempts to start the vehicle, the engine is prevented from starting, reducing theft and unauthorised use.

Question 5 of 10

Would you like deep integration with your vehicles' onboard systems to capture live engine data and diagnostics?

CAN Bus Integration taps directly into each vehicle's data network , providing real-time engine diagnostics, fuel consumption, RPM, odometer readings and fault code alerts.

Question 6 of 10

Do you need real-time cargo weight monitoring to manage load compliance and protect against cargo theft?

Cargo Protect Weight Management monitors your vehicle's load weight continuously , alerting you to overloading and unexpected weight changes, and helping maintain legal compliance.

Question 7 of 10

Does your fleet include concrete mixer vehicles that require drum rotation monitoring?

The Cement Rotation Sensor monitors concrete mixer drum rotation in real time: verifying mixing compliance, detecting operational irregularities, reducing waste and ensuring delivery quality on every job site.

Question 8 of 10

Would you like video recording capabilities on your fleet vehicles?

Dashcam solutions give you visual evidence of accidents and incidents, livestreaming, remote footage requests and continuous recording all integrated with your telematics.

Question 9 of 10

Do you need AI-powered real-time driver coaching, fatigue detection and safety monitoring?

AI camera solutions actively monitor driver behaviour in real time , alerting to drowsiness, phone usage, seatbelt violations and lane departure.

Question 10 of 10

How many camera angles do you need per vehicle?

More angles give you full visibility, blind spot elimination and internal cabin monitoring.
